A note on translation

Why the full documents remain in French.

The PAMOJA project operates primarily in Côte d'Ivoire, a francophone country. All fieldwork, interviews, partner materials, and briefing documents are developed in French to preserve nuance and cultural accuracy. Translating 150+ pages of working documents into English without domain-specific SBC expertise would introduce imprecision in areas where precision matters most (local idioms, nuance around faith and family, strategic language choices).

This English-language overview site exists so international funders, partners, and researchers can understand the framework and engage with the methodology. Full document translation will be considered in a later phase, based on demand and available resources.
